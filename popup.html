About
Simple Policy breaks down companies' complex privacy policies into short summaries.
Methodology
We gather our information from directly from the sources' websites; however, our summaries do not account for all the nuances of the companies' privacy policies. If you find any mistakes, or any ratings you don't agree with, please contact us at support@openlens.net.
